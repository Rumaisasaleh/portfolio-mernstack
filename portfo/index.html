About



I am Deepika Padukone, born on 5 January 1986, an Indian actress primarily working in Hindi films. As of 2023, I am India's highest-paid actress, and my accolades include three Filmfare Awards. I am often listed among the nation's most popular personalities; in 2018, Time named me one of the 100 most influential people in the world, and in 2022, I received the Time100 Impact Award.


I was born in Copenhagen and raised in Bangalore, the daughter of badminton player Prakash Padukone. During my teenage years, I played badminton in national-level championships but eventually left my sports career to pursue fashion modeling. This transition led to film offers, and I made my acting debut in 2006 as the title character of the Kannada film *Aishwarya*. My first Bollywood release was the romance *Om Shanti Om* (2007), where I played a dual role opposite Shah Rukh Khan, earning me the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut. I received praise for my starring role in the romance *Love Aaj Kal* (2009), although my career experienced a brief setback afterward.
